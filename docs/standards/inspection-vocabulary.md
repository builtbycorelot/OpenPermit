# Inspection Vocabulary

Standardized inspection types and result codes ensure interoperable scheduling, reporting, and auditing across jurisdictions.

## InspectionType Examples
- `inspection.footing`
- `inspection.foundation`
- `inspection.framing`
- `inspection.rough_electrical`
- `inspection.rough_plumbing`
- `inspection.rough_mechanical`
- `inspection.insulation`
- `inspection.final_building`
- `inspection.final_trade`

## Result Codes
- `pass`
- `fail`
- `partial`
- `cancelled`
- `reschedule_required`
